Business Recommendations
------------------------
High Priority
- Prioritize retention outreach for customers with the highest churn probability. This helps teams focus retention effort where revenue risk is most concentrated.
- Launch early-tenure onboarding support during the first 12 months. This matters because short-tenure customers show elevated churn risk and may need faster value realization.
- Promote annual contract upgrades for month-to-month customers. This can reduce churn exposure by encouraging stronger customer commitment.

Medium Priority
- Review pricing and value perception for high monthly charge customers. This matters because higher monthly charges may increase churn exposure when customers do not clearly see service value.
- Use targeted offers for customers with limited service bundles. This can improve retention by aligning offers with customer needs and service adoption patterns.

Supporting Priority
- Monitor churn risk before renewal and billing-cycle milestones. This helps identify potential churn risk before customers make switching decisions.
- Refresh retention strategy as feature importance patterns change. This keeps business actions aligned with the most current churn drivers identified by the model.
